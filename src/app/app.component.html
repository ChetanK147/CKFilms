About Me
Presnting stories along with stunning visuals is my passion

I am a passionate cinematographer always on the lookout for challenging and thought-provoking stories to tell. I consider myself to be a story first DP and I believe my lighting design and camera techniques are, in addition to setting a tone, meant to establish character, further the script's story and help execute the director's vision. I consider myself to be a storyteller first and a cinematographer second. As for my style, I enjoy embracing natural light, like staying away from conventional filmmaking techniques, and will never shy away from a challenge. Whether it's a long handheld shot or a static composed shot, I approach everything with the same attention to detail. I worked my way up through both the camera and lighting departments and know that my background gives me the professional and creative skills to work within any budget and most time frames. And I pride myself on this. In addition, I own a full BMPCC6K package with a set of DZOFILMS Pictor Zooms & a Tokina Cine Ultrawide Zoom. You will have a hard time finding other DP's that are as passionate as I am. I love what I do and hope to get the chance to work with you.
